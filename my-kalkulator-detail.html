My Kalkulator

Category: App
Created: 2025

My Kalkulator is a calculator app that can open a secret folder. Just like a secret calculator, this app will automatically open the secret folder when the result matches the predefined secret number.

To keep the secret folder hidden, I use Command Prompt to hide the folder. Even if we use "Show hidden files", the folder still remains invisible.
