Dark Land

Category: Game
Created: 2025

Dark Land is a 2D platformer game that I created as my first project for practice and a school assignment. Most of the assets in this game were taken from the Unity Asset Store and Pixabay.

The game tells the story of a miner working for a dark corporation. He is placed in an underground facility and is forced to work. He cannot escape because the entrance and exit are guarded by a butcher named Banken. One day, he decides to leave, and guided by an AI, he must defeat enemies and navigate a confusing maze.

You can play this game by visiting my Itch.io page. It is available for both Desktop and Android.
